# Disclaimer

IP Breaker is a pre-launch intellectual property risk triage and red-team analysis tool.

It does **not** provide legal opinions, legal advice, freedom-to-operate opinions, infringement opinions, validity opinions, clearance opinions, or trademark registrability opinions.

IP Breaker is designed to help builders identify potential risk signals before launch, including:

- Open-source license and attribution issues
- Possible code clone or copyright risk signals
- Possible product name, logo, or slogan collision risks
- UI or design lookalike risk signals
- Patent claim trap or freedom-to-operate review triggers

All findings are preliminary risk signals. They should be reviewed by qualified intellectual property counsel before commercial launch, fundraising, public distribution, or enforcement-related decisions.

The Casper attestation component records hashes and risk metadata only. It does not certify that a product is non-infringing, legally safe, or cleared for launch.

Users are responsible for verifying all results, checking applicable jurisdictions, and seeking professional advice where appropriate.
